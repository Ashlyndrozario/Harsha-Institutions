Course Overview Life at Harsha

The Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) is an undergraduate course that covers topics like financial accounting, corporate tax, economics, company law, auditing, business management, and financial law. The course emphasizes on the application of the concepts of B.Com in the effective and efficient planning strategies included in the management, maintenance, and transport of goods to maintain consistent business.
