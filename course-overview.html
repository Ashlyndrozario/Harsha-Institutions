Discover Your Career Path with Us

Explore the various job positions available and plan your future.

(0-2 years)

Logistics Assistant/Analyst

Start as a Logistics Assistant or Supply Chain Analyst, assisting with coordination, data analysis, and process improvement initiatives.

(2-4 years)

Inventory Control Specialist

Transition to a role as an Inventory Control Specialist, focusing on optimizing inventory levels, managing stock movements, and implementing inventory management systems.

(4-6 years)

Operations Coordinator/Supervisorere

Progress to an Operations Coordinator or Supervisor position, where you'll oversee day-to-day logistics operations, manage teams, and ensure smooth warehouse and transportation activities.

(6-8 years)

Supply Chain Planner/Manager

Advance to a role as a Supply Chain Planner or Manager, responsible for strategic planning, demand forecasting, supplier relationship management, and overall supply chain optimization.

(8+ years)

Director of Logistics/VP of Supply Chain

Reach executive levels as a Director of Logistics or Vice President of Supply Chain, leading the strategic direction of the supply chain function, driving efficiency, and contributing to overall business strategy.
